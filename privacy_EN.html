Ningshen Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we handle and protect your data in Ningshen (the “App”). We are designed with the principle of collecting only the data necessary for the features to work.

1. Summary

  • No collection of personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email address, or phone number.
  • No ads or third‑party tracking. We do not embed ad or tracking SDKs.
  • Training records and statistics are stored on your device and used for local features and visualization.
  • In‑app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.
  • Delete your data anytime via in‑app options (if provided) or by uninstalling the App.

2. Data we collect or process

Category Examples Purpose Linked to you Shared externally
On‑device training data Training history, score trends, tap rhythm, reaction times, heatmaps, etc. Generate on‑device statistics and visualizations to support training and review No (stored on device only) No
Purchase status Subscription validity, plan type (weekly/monthly/yearly/family sharing) Unlock Pro features and verify entitlements No (validated via StoreKit) No (payments handled by Apple)

Note: The App does not require an account and does not collect sensitive data such as contacts, location, photos, microphone, or address book. When sharing training cards, content is generated locally, and sharing is your choice via the system share sheet.

3. How we use data

  • Compute training statistics and visualizations on device (e.g., trend charts, heatmaps, rhythm charts).
  • Validate subscriptions and unlock Pro features (including Family Sharing).
  • Improve stability (when you consent to share diagnostics with Apple).

4. Data storage and retention

  • Training and history data are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not store your personal data on our own servers.
  • Deletion: clear data in the App (if provided) or uninstall the App to remove local data.

5. Data sharing and third parties

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third parties.
  • Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple’s App Store / StoreKit; we cannot access your card details.
  • We do not integrate third‑party ad or tracking SDKs.

6. Children

This App is intended for general audiences and is not primarily directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information unintentionally, please contact us using the methods below so we can address it.

7. Your rights

  • Access and correction: You can view and manage your training data within the App.
  • Deletion: Clear data in the App (if provided) or uninstall the App to delete local data.
  • Withdraw permissions: You can disable permissions such as notifications in system settings.

8. Subscriptions and trials

  • After any trial period, subscriptions renew automatically based on the selected billing cycle. You can cancel anytime in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Family Sharing (if applicable) is managed by Apple, and eligible family members can share subscription benefits.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as features or regulations change. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised date. For material changes, we will notify you in the App or through other reasonable means.
